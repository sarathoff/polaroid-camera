## Easy Creation Methods

### Method 1: Online Favicon Generator (Easiest)
1. Go to **https://favicon.io/**
2. Choose "Text" or "Image" option
3. For Text: Enter "üì∑" or "PB"
4. Set background color to #C39974
5. Download all sizes
6. Upload to your website root

### Method 2: Canva (Free)
1. Go to **https://canva.com**
2. Create custom size: 512x512px
3. Add camera emoji or design
4. Download as PNG
5. Use online tool to resize to other sizes

### Method 3: Figma (Free)
1. Create 512x512px artboard
2. Design your icon
3. Export as PNG at different sizes
4. Upload to website

### Method 4: Use Emoji as Favicon
1. Go to **https://favicon.io/emoji-favicons/**
2. Search for "camera" üì∑
3. Download the favicon package
4. Extract and upload files

## Pro Tips

1. **Keep it simple**: Favicons are tiny, avoid complex details
2. **High contrast**: Ensure icon is visible on light and dark backgrounds
3. **Test at small sizes**: View at 16x16 to ensure clarity
4. **Use PNG**: Better quality than ICO format
5. **Transparent background**: Works better across different contexts

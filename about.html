About Polaroid Booth

Your free online polaroid camera and vintage photo editor

What is Polaroid Booth?

Polaroid Booth is a free, web-based application that brings the nostalgic charm of instant polaroid cameras to the digital age. Create stunning vintage-style photos directly in your browser - no downloads, no installations, no hassle.

Whether you're capturing moments with your webcam or uploading existing photos, Polaroid Booth transforms them into beautiful, retro polaroid-style images complete with customizable filters, effects, and handwritten-style text.

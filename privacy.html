How It Works

Polaroid Booth is a 100% client-side application. This means:

  • All photo processing happens directly in your web browser
  • Your photos stay on your device at all times
  • When you close the browser, everything is gone
  • No data is transmitted to our servers

Camera Access

When you use the camera feature:

  • We request camera permission through your browser
  • The camera feed is processed locally in your browser
  • No video or images are recorded or transmitted
  • You can revoke camera access at any time through your browser settings

Downloaded Photos

When you download a photo:

  • The photo is saved directly to your device
  • We don't keep a copy
  • The download happens entirely in your browser

Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics to understand:

  • How many people visit the site
  • Which features are most popular
  • General geographic regions (country-level only)

These analytics are anonymous and don't track individual users or their photos.

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts for typography (no tracking)
  • Basic hosting infrastructure

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use Polaroid Booth without providing any personal information
  • Deny camera access (you can still upload photos)
  • Clear your browser cache and local storage at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Polaroid Booth is safe for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there's no special data collection for children.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 23, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Polaroid Booth is designed with privacy as a top priority. All photo processing happens directly in your browser. Your photos never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Photos and Images: When you use Polaroid Booth, all photos captured or uploaded are processed entirely in your web browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos to any server.

Camera Access: If you choose to use the camera feature, we request access to your device's camera. This access is used solely for capturing photos within the application and is never used for any other purpose.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views and feature usage to improve the application. This data does not include any personal information or photos.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Polaroid Booth service
  • To improve and optimize the user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Client-Side Processing: All photo editing and processing occurs in your browser using JavaScript. Your photos are stored temporarily in your browser's memory and are automatically cleared when you close the tab or clear your browser cache.

No Server Upload: We do not upload your photos to any server. When you download a photo, it is saved directly from your browser to your device.

Camera Permissions: Camera access is requested only when you click the "Start Camera" button and is used exclusively for capturing photos within the application.

4. Third-Party Services

Polaroid Booth uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google Fonts may collect usage data according to their privacy policy.

We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party services that track user behavior.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Polaroid Booth may use browser local storage to save your preferences (such as filter settings) for a better user experience. This data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use Polaroid Booth without providing any personal information
  • Deny camera access (you can still upload photos)
  • Clear your browser cache and local storage at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

Polaroid Booth is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all processing happens client-side, no personal data is collected from any users, including children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
